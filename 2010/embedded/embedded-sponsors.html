Embedded sponsors

FSCONS Embedded is held for the first time 2010 after having been a part of the general FSCONS since 2007. With FSCONS Embedded we hope to give the vivid market and the numerous developers and companies a place to meet in the Nordic countries.

The schedule is not finished yet, so there's still room for you and your company if you want to talk or present something. Just drop us a proposal by emailing us at emb [at] fscons.org. A complete list of talks is availbale here: http://fscons.org/upcoming/embedded Already was have great speakers and interesting topics, including:

Alessandro Rubini - Data structures benchmarked in the Linux kernel

Alessandro is the primary author of the de-facto standard Device Driver book by O'Reilly. Alessandro is currently working as a University teacher and develops high end embedded software.

Mikael Söderberg - GENIVI alliance and how biz can adapt FOSS

Mikael is the CTO of Pelagicore and chair of the GENIVI Alliance Reference System Work Group.

Javier Serrano - Open Hardware Repository

Javier is the founder of Open Hardware Repository and lead of the hardware and timing section at CERN.

By sponsoring FSCONS Embedded you can show that your company supports the FOSS community in the Nordic region and you can make new connections in the relaxed atmosphere that is provided at FSCONS.

There are various ways to sponsor us and we're always open for discussions so don't hesitate to contact us.

FSCONS Embedded - http://fscons.org/embedded

Best regards, FSCONS Embedded team

by Richard Stallman

Following from his previous presentations in Stockholm and Göteborg, Richard Stallman will sit down together with Laura Creighton to talk about free software. They will speak about the ability for free software to transform society by bringing freedom to their computers, the path that the GNU project and Richard embarked on almost 30 years ago, the problems that were faced and eventually overcome, as well as the pitfalls that we're likely to face in the years to come.

It is recommended that before attending this session you familiarise yourself with the topic of free software by attending one of Richard Stallmans presentations in Stockholm or Göteborg. You will also find much relevant information on the following links:

  • http://www.gnu.org/philosophy/philosophy.html
  • http://www.gnu.org/philosophy/free-sw.html
  • http://www.gnu.org/philosophy/words-to-avoid.html
  • http://www.gnu.org/philosophy/open-source-misses-the-point.html
  • http://www.gnu.org/gnu/gnu-linux-faq.html
  • http://www.gnu.org/gnu/the-gnu-project.html

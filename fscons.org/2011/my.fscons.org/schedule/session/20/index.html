Workshop: Arduino workshop for women

Day
Friday 11th of November, 2011
Start
12:00
End
17:00
Duration
5:00:00
Room
K333 (Språkbanken)

by Christina Gratorp & Hedvig Kamp

Prerequisites: None Need to bring to workshop: Computer Time: 4 hours Goal: To show that Arduino hacking isn't that difficult, get more women interested in computing.

Content: We will give a workshop on how to get started with Arduino. The workshop will include setting up an appropriate environment (Ardiuno and Processing) and make a small application for playing different tones by pressing different buttons. The necessary hardware (except computer) will be provided by us. The workshop will be for women only.

Christina Gratorp & Hedvig Kamp

As one of the goal of the workshop is to get more women interested in computing, this workshop is for women only. One of the reasons for this is to allow the women participating to feel at home and not singled out as a minority. If you're interested in a talk on Arduino open for everyone, please see our Arduino talk by David Cuartielles

In order for us to be able to plan the workshop sessions and which rooms to put them in, the deadline for letting us know about your attendence of any of the workshops are October 28th.

Claudia Rauch

Biography

Claudia is the business manager of KDE e.V., the non-profit organisation supporting the KDE community. In this capacity, she has organised many events from small developer meetings and workshop to large conferences with several hundred attendees, like Akademy and the Desktop Summit. She is also responsible for fundraising and general community support. She is passionate about connecting people and organising events.

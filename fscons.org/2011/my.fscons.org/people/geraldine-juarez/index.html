Biography

I am interested in exploring the configurations that arise when you add the Internet factor to traditional concepts of society, information, property, culture and governance. My work deals with these issues through different outputs and activities related to art, hacking, blogging and lately, civic trolling! I am a member of F.A.T. LAB and a blogger for ALT1040 and Global Voices.

Sam Tuke

Biography

Sam is a writer, software developer and designer. He is currently the Free Software Foundation Europe's UK coordinator, based in Manchester, and also FSFE's editorial team coordinator. In 2010 he organised FSFE's "Decentralising Internet communications track at FSCONS. He has a background in philosophy, which he studied at University College London, has been a GNU/Linux user and advocate since 2003, and works part time as a web application engineer.

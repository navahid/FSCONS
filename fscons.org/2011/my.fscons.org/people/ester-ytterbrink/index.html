Ester Ytterbrink

Biography

I am a software developer and geek who did some studying and a bit of teaching at Certec, the Division of Rehabilitation Engineering Research in the Department of Design Sciences, Faculty of Engineering at Lund University. Now I work for a consultancy company in Malmö. Technology should be fun, for the user and for those involved in producing it. The rest of the world is enough of a challenge to us for code to be left ugly or software hard to use. Since I am visual impaired myself, a crip, I have thought a bit on how the society and technology can work together to enable or disable people.

Sandra Derbring

Biography

Sandra Derbring is a computational linguist at DART, a regional rehab center for people with communication disorders. She works in different projects, particularly with graphical symbol ontologies and concept coding. She is for example a part of the AEGIS project, that aims at making desktop and mobile environments accessible for people with disabilities.

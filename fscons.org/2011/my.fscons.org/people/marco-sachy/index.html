Marco Sachy

Biography

Marco 'Radium' Sachy is a currency designer for Transition management. At EIPE - Rotterdam, his MA thesis resulted in a philosophical assessment of the structural shortcomings of a system exclusively implementing modern bank money. He is currently conducting Post-graduate research at University of Leicester's School of Management around the conditions of possibility for the development of a resilient G/Local multi-currency system. Out of the academy, he is an activist developing DYNDY: spreading the message of conscious money alongside, and by means of, Free Open Source Software.

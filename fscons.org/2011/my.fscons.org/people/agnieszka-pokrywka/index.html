Agnieszka Pokrywka

Biography

Agnieszka Pokrywka performs several roles as an artist, film maker, graphic designer, researcher although formally she is educated in art criticism, animated film, and computer simulations. Nowadays she is PhD candidate in the field of open source software and art at the Norwegian University of Science and Technology (Computer and Information Science) in Trondheim.

In her artistic creativity she rather uses digital media. She took part in over 40 collective exhibitions and film festivals world wide.

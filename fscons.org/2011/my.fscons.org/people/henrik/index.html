Henrik Sandklef

FSCONS Staff
Biography

One of the founders of FSCONS, and have thus been volunteering since 2007. At FSCONS I try to do as much as I can, which is probably really close to nothing if you ask Rikard and Jonas. I manage the Development in Free Software Communities or Development for Embedded Systems tracks. I try my best to do more of overall planning for FSCONS given the knowledge I have from the previous conferences.

When not doing FSCONS I work as a teacher at Chalmers University of technology and Gothenburg University. I am working for and have been a member of FSFE since 2005 and the GNU project since 2001.

I have my own company: Sandklef GNU Labs, which is part of Fossgruppen

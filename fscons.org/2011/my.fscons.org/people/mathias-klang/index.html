Mathias Klang

Biography

Mathias currently works as a researcher and senior lecturer at the University of Göteborg and the University of Borås. His research revolves within the field of legal informatics with particular interest in copyright, online expression, digital rights and regulation. He is currently researching these areas in relation to Social Media. He regularly lectures in these areas and has published several articles. Among his work is a co-edited volume (with Andrew Murray) entitled “Human Rights in the Digital Age” and his PhD thesis “Disruptive Technology: effects of technology regulation on democracy”. In addition to this, Mathias is the Project Lead for Creative Commons Sweden.

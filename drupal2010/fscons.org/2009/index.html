6-7th of November 2010

Join the planning on http://wiki.fscons.org/wiki/FSCONS_2010_Planning


Join us for the fourth annual FSCONS, a meeting place for social change, focused on the future of free software and free society. Open discussion and brainstorming are two of the primary factors that pave the way for change. In FSCONS, we seek to engage, inform, sometimes provoke, and always motivate social change through these discussions.

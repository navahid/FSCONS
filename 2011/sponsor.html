Sponsoring FSCONS

There are many ways to sponsor FSCONS: signing up for one of the sponsorship deals below, buying business tickets for your employees, or giving a cash donation at the time you buy your own ticket for the conference. You can also consider sponsoring with technology or services. Please get in touch with our conference manager, Jonas Öberg at +46-31-7802161 or jonas@ffkp.se to discuss the options available.

Sponsorship deals

Bronze / Patron 5000 SEK

  • Your name and logo in the program booklet
  • Your name and logo in the sponsorship section of the web site
  • One free admission to the conference
  • Space in the lounge area to place brochures

Silver / Supporting 10000 SEK

  • Same as Bronze, and
  • One additional free admissions (total of 2)
  • Your name and logo also on the registration page
  • Space in a shared booth to present your organisation

Gold / Associate 20000 SEK

  • Same as Silver, and
  • Two additional free admissions (total of 4)
  • Ability to include information/material in a bag given to participants
  • Your name and logo on the program page
  • Space for a booth to present your organisation

Platinum / Executive 40000 SEK

  • Same as Gold, and
  • Four additional free admissions (total of 8)
  • Space for a rollup in the conference venue main hall

Media partner

If you are a publication or media outlet, you are welcome to contact us with any proposal for partnering with FSCONS. We are open for suggestions and have previously partnered with several magazines.

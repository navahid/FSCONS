Andy Wingo

Biography

Since late 2009, I co-maintain guile. I maintain the Guile bindings to the GNOME stack of libraries. I've hacked on GStreamer a bit in the past (pre-0.8 times), and quite a bit in the 0.8 to 0.10 transition. It was the first collaborative free software project I got involved in. I feel like I really came of age there.

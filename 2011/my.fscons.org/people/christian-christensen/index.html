Christian Christensen

Biography

Christian Christensen is Professor of Media and Communications Studies in the Department of Informatics and Media at Uppsala University, Sweden. Christensen is the editor or co-editor of a number of forthcoming books, including "Twitter Revolutions? Political Activism and Dissent in an Age of Social Media" (2012; New York: Peter Lang), "Online Territories: Mediated Practice and Social Space" (2011; New York: Peter Lang) and has an edited volume on WikiLeaks in production. He is also the guest editor a special issue (2011) of the journal The Communication Review entitled, "Twitter Revolutions?Addressing Social Media and Dissent."

Biography

Francisco Blas Izquierdo Riera (Klondike)

Klondike's main mission is getting fscons talks streamed out to the world.

He tried to get this job done during FSCONS 2010 after seeing a notice posted a week before the venue but flu made it impossible for him to even try to get near to the events nor the organizers. He approached the organization again in September with the proposal after being redirected to the meeting in the #fscons channel by wildcard.

Aside of this, he is studying the computer security Master in Chalmers in Sweden and doing his project to get the Computer Engineer degree in the UPV in Spain, he is also a Gentoo developer (without access to the portage tree) where he does bug handling, assembler PIC fixing, doc writing, PR and evangelizing for the Gentoo Hardened project. He is also known for mounting Direct Connect servers at various lan parties in Spain and Sweden, including Campus Party Valencia, and for being the network responsible at the Infor & Games Festival in Castellon. He also was a developer of the now unmaintained Kontinuidad Jabata project, where he adquired his fear of QT.

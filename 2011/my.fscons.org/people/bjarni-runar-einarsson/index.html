Bjarni Rúnar Einarsson

Biography

Bjarni Rúnar Einarsson has been one of Iceland's most prominent Software Freedom supporters and Consumer Digital Rights advocates since the mid-90s, receiving the Nordic Free Software Award at FSCONS 2010 for his efforts. Professionally Bjarni has worked as a software developer, a spam- and virus fighter (FRISK Software) and systems administrator (Icelandic ISPs and Google Ireland). Today Bjarni works full time on PageKite, trying to build a business based on the ideals of Free Software.

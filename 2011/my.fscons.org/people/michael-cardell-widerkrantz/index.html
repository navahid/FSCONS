Biography

My name is Michael Cardell Widerkrantz. People usually call me MC.

I live in the south of Sweden with my wife Petra and three kids in ages from toddler to teenagers. We share a flat with a lot of computers and at least eleven bookcases.

I started programming computers in 1985. In 1995 people started paying me to do it. Most of my programming concerns Unix systems and computer networks. See résumé and hacks.

Important (in no particular order): Books, free software, Emacs, FreeBSD, mechanical keyboards, the end-to-end principle, IPv6, strong coffee, human liberation, animal liberation, vegan food, scientific scepticism, zen buddhism.

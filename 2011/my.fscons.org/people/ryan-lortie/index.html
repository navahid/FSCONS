Ryan Lortie

Biography

Living on the beautiful shores of Lake Ontario, in Mimico, Ontario (near Toronto), Ryan is a professional GNOME developer. He spends his days working for Codethink Limited (a small consultancy based in the UK), hacking on GNOME. Ryan has been involved with the GNOME project for a bit over half a decade and is presently the maintainer of GLib. He also sits on the board of directors of The GNOME Foundation.

If Ryan ever won the lottery, he'd quit his job and continue to do the same work he's doing now.

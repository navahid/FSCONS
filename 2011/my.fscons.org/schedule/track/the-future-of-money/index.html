The Future of Money

One of FSCONS' tracks

The Future of Money goes beyond the current economic system and looks at emerging radical ways of handling transactions, trade and where it all might lead us. Should the exchange of money be decentralised? Localised? Optional? Trust-based? Or should we work towards eliminating the need for money altogether?

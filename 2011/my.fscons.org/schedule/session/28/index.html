Free Software in Politics

by Daniel Melin

How far has the Swedish public sector climbed the mountain "Kings of Free Software usage"? Who is using Free Software today? What kinds of Free Software are popular? What about support? Has the Swedish public sector started climbing at all?

These are all questions that could make you feel uneasy. So, will this talk make you happy again? Only one way to find out!
